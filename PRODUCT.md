# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Donors, adults, high schoolers, and investors visiting the Aveira Clocks landing page. Donors and investors come to evaluate the project's legitimacy and ambition; adults and high schoolers come to learn about the product and team.

## Product Purpose

Aveira Clocks is a student-built smart clock company blending elegant design with modern technology. The landing page exists to present the product, build trust in the team of four high school founders, and drive donor/investor support for prototyping and manufacturing.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, modern, clean. Confident but understated — premium without being flashy. The brand speaks with the quiet authority of good design, letting the product photography and clean layout do the talking.

## Anti-references

Generic AI-designed templates, dark/gaming tech aesthetic, cluttered layouts, loud gradients, aggressive CTAs, over-designed hero sections, and anything that feels mass-produced rather than carefully crafted.

## Design Principles

- **Time, beautifully designed** — the site must reflect the same care as the product. Every element earns its place.
- **Minimalist confidence** — generous whitespace, no clutter, let the product imagery breathe.
- **Premium simplicity** — feels high-end without being flashy. Rounded corners, clean typography, soft palette.
- **Show the work** — product photography and team profiles are the stars; text supports, never competes.
- **Youthful ambition** — made by high school students, executed with professional quality. Honest about being a student project without apologizing for it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Reduced motion preference respected throughout. WCAG AA contrast minimum.
